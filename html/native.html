nativity

"A complex native plant landscape welcomes wildlife by offering sheltered nesting spaces and fostering a local food web that provides resources throughout the seasons." 1

"Native plant materials are advantageous because they:

  1. Are unlikely to be invasive or overly competitive with other native plants
  2. Return to or maintain more normal fire intervals and fuel loadings
  3. Provide food sources (nectar, pollen, seeds, leaves, and stems) for native butterflies, insects, birds, and other animals
  4. Reduce energy consumption and pollution (limited need for mowing)
  5. Reduce the need for pesticides
  6. Enhance aesthetics and visual quality
  7. Protect at-risk species
  8. Protect biodiversity and stewardship of our natural heritage" 2
